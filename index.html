01 / How it works

As your business runs, agents keep the program moving.

You don't have to start the work or remember when it's due. Agents watch what's happening in the program, recognize what needs to happen next, and prepare the documents before the deadline arrives. The paperwork is ready when you are.

01 / Watch

Agents catch what would otherwise live in someone's head

Hours, milestones, wage steps, reporting windows, and deadlines change constantly. Audacity tracks them as they happen, so nothing waits on someone remembering it first.

02 / Decide

Agents know what needs to happen next

An apprentice becomes eligible for a raise. An audit window opens. A grant deadline approaches. Audacity recognizes the trigger and starts the right workflow on its own.

03 / Execute

Agents bring back finished work

Records pulled, forms prepared, packets assembled, filings drafted. The work shows up ready for review, not as another task on your list.

02 / What Audacity handles

The complex parts of running a program, handled in the background.

These are the recurring operational jobs that usually require someone to track dates, chase records, fill forms, and keep the program defensible. Agents keep them moving as part of the system.
